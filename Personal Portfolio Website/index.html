It is an important part of every school and college, and it helps the librarian to keep records of available books as well as issued books. The Online Library Management System is a sophisticated and indispensable automated solution designed to streamline and enhance library operations. It serves as a comprehensive tool for the efficient management of libraries across educational institutions, including schools and colleges. This system empowers librarians by providing a centralized platform to maintain meticulous records of available books and those that have been borrowed. It automates tasks such as book cataloging, tracking due dates, and managing inventory.
